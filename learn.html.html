Lesson 1: Numbers

Here are the numbers from 1 to 10 in French:

  • 1 - un
  • 2 - deux
  • 3 - trois
  • 4 - quatre
  • 5 - cinq
  • 6 - six
  • 7 - sept
  • 8 - huit
  • 9 - neuf
  • 10 - dix

Practice writing these numbers and try using them in sentences!

Lesson 9: Basic Questions

Here are some basic questions in French:
